Frequently asked questions

What is Clinekt for primary care practices?

Clinekt is an AI patient acquisition and re-engagement platform for primary care practices. Its AI agents engage new patients from your website and phone, re-engage overdue patients from your EHR, fill provider panels, and book confirmed appointments into your scheduler, across phone, text, and email, 24/7.

What's the best AI for primary care patient acquisition and scheduling?

The best AI for a primary care practice handles both sides of growth: activating new patients and re-engaging existing ones. Clinekt answers and screens new-patient inquiries, books them by visit type and provider, and mines your EHR to rebook wellness, chronic-care, and lapsed patients, all written back to your EHR.

Can Clinekt answer primary care patient calls, texts, and web inquiries 24/7?

Yes. Clinekt's AI agents answer primary care calls, texts, and website inquiries around the clock, eliminating missed calls, hold times, and after-hours gaps, and book qualified patients directly into your schedule.

How does Clinekt handle primary care scheduling and routing?

Clinekt screens each inquiry by reason for visit and insurance, routes it to the right provider, visit type, and location, and books it into open slots, including wellness, sick, and chronic-care visits in your EHR.

Can Clinekt re-engage lapsed and overdue primary care patients from our EHR?

Yes. Clinekt mines your EHR for patients overdue for an annual wellness visit, a chronic-care follow-up, or a recommended screening and reaches out across phone, text, and email to rebook them.

How is Clinekt different from an answering service or a scheduling tool?

Answering services take messages and scheduling tools wait for patients to act. Clinekt's AI agents actively activate new patients and re-engage existing ones, screening, answering, and booking appointments end to end, 24/7, without adding front-desk staff.

Which EHRs does Clinekt integrate with?

Clinekt integrates with the major primary care EHRs, including Epic, athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, NextGen, and Elation, among others. If you don't see your system, book a demo and we'll confirm support.

Is Clinekt HIPAA compliant?

Yes. Clinekt is built for healthcare environments and handles protected health information in accordance with HIPAA. See our Trust Center for details.
