FAQ

Common questions from oral surgery practices.

How does Clinekt screen oral surgery patients?

The Inbound Agent runs a specialty-trained screening covering procedure interest, referral status, pain level, timeline, and insurance or financing questions. Your scheduling team receives a complete patient profile, not a contact form.

Does it work with our EHR?

Yes. Clinekt works alongside Dolphin, Denticon, WinOMS, OMSVision, Curve Dental, and the other major systems. Your team books appointments exactly the way they do today, with no IT project and no workflow changes.

How fast can we go live?

Most practices go live in under a week. Setup is a single embed on your website, followed by a short review of your providers, services, and scheduling rules.

What does the front desk have to change?

Nothing. Clinekt hands your team pre-screened, booking-ready patients. The workflow stays the same; the inputs get dramatically better.

How do we know it's working?

Every patient is traced from first touch to completed procedure. You see leads, bookings, show rates, and completed care in one report, with each one attributed to its source.
